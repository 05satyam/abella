Preservation of strong normalization for a variant of the λσ-calculus

by Anders Schack-Nielsen

The λσ-calculus is an explicit substitution calculus for the λ-calculus. Paul-André Mellies has shown that a strongly normalizing term in the λ-calculus is not necessarily strongly normalizing in the λσ-calculus. In the current work, Anders Schack-Nielsen has shown that a simple restriction on the λσ-calculus recovers this preservation of strong normalization property. Schack-Nielsen has formalized this result in Abella and reported the results in this tech report which will be included in his forthcoming Ph.D. thesis.
